About Likhang CHAS

Our Mission

To empower CHAS students and organizations by providing a user-friendly, centralized e-commerce platform for purchasing department merchandise, streamlining transactions, promoting department branding, and supporting local student businesses, ultimately fostering a sense of community pride and strengthening ties within the CHAS department.

Our Vision

To become the preferred and most convenient platform for all nursing students, faculty, and alumni at the College of Health and Allied Sciences to access and purchase high-quality, department-branded merchandise, while supporting student entrepreneurs and fostering a vibrant and engaged community within the CHAS department.

History and Background

Likhang CHAS started as a draft to solve one of the many inconveniences students from the CHAS department were experiencing- its reliance on multiple, inefficient Google Forms. Recognizing this need, a group of dedicated students from level 2 took the initiative to develop "Likhang CHAS," a centralized e-commerce platform. This platform aimed to streamline transactions, provide a single point of access for merchandise, and simplify the buying and selling process for everyone involved. The idea was shed to light by the chairman of the project and within days, it was eventually agreed upon by the whole class of 2BSN-C. Immediately, it was proposed to the dean of the department, Dean Beunaseda who had a couple of suggestions. Through various revisions and the committee heads and their members working together, Likhang CHAS was made and approved by the dean, faculty, and organizations they are working with. All that to say, Likhang CHAS is a student-led organization built to help target customers, buy and enjoy their desired merchandise to show off their school spirit! The Likhang CHAS team is an optimistic and gratitude-filled group of level 2 nursing students dedicated to creating services customers will use and love.

Our Purpose Is To Creatively Craft and Deliver Exclusive CHAS Merchandise That Embodies Pride, Quality, and Tradition.
